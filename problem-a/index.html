Iron Man

Iron man is a fictinoal character introduced in an American superhero film Iron Man in 2008. He is a wealthy American business magnate and scientist "Tony" Stark, who suffers from a severe chest injury during a kidnapping. And later, he develops his suit and weapon devices he designed through his company, Stark Industries.
